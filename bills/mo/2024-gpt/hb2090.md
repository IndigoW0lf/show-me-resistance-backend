summary_short: "This bill updates rules about the Neighborhood Assistance Act tax credits to improve funding and eligibility."

summary: |
  This bill makes changes to how the state of Missouri manages tax credits for community assistance programs. It clarifies the order in which different businesses, like insurance companies and banks, can apply these credits against their taxes. The bill also sets new limits on the total amount of tax credits available each year and specifies criteria for communities that qualify for higher credits. These updates aim to encourage more contributions to local community projects, especially in smaller or rural areas, benefiting local residents and organizations.
