summary: |
  The bill updates the current laws on campaign finance to explicitly permit candidates and campaign committees to receive contributions and make payments using mobile payment services, such as apps and electronic transfers. This makes it easier for campaigns to handle money digitally and stays current with modern payment methods. The law also clarifies definitions related to candidates, campaigns, and financial transactions to improve transparency and ensure proper reporting. It affects political candidates, campaign committees, and election officials by providing clearer rules for digital financial activities.
