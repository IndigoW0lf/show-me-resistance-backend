summary_short: "This bill exempts certain farm vehicles from emissions inspection requirements in Missouri."
summary: |
  The bill changes existing rules about vehicle emissions inspections in Missouri. It exempts specific farm vehicles from needing regular emissions tests, especially those used only on farms or with certain characteristics. This means that some farm trucks and machinery will no longer have to go through emissions checks when they are sold or registered. Overall, it aims to reduce regulatory burdens on farmers and farm-related vehicles.
