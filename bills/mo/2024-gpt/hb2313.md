  This bill establishes the STEM Grant program, which provides financial assistance to students studying in science, technology, engineering, or mathematics (STEM) fields at approved Missouri institutions. Eligible students who receive this grant can get up to $500 per award, with a maximum of $2,000 in total. The program is designed to help both new students and those already receiving financial aid, as long as they continue to meet academic progress standards.
