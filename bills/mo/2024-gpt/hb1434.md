summary: |
  This bill changes the rules governing cosmetology, including who is considered a cosmetologist and how they are trained and licensed. It defines different types of cosmetology work, such as hairdressing, manicuring, and esthetician services. The goal is to clarify licenses and training requirements to ensure safe and professional beauty services. It will affect cosmetologists, students in cosmetology schools, and the state board responsible for licensing and regulation.
