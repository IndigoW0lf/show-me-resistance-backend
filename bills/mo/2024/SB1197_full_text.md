AN ACT
To amend chapter 42, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to Missouri veterans'
homes.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 42, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 42.137, to read as
3 follows:
1 42.137. State employees in support care assistance
2 positions for Missouri veterans' homes shall receive a wage
3 in the amount of at least fifteen dollars per hour and no
4 more than twenty-two dollars per hour for services performed
5 for a Missouri veterans' home. Such wage shall be effective
6 on January 1, 2025. Any state employee in a support care
7 assistance position for Missouri veterans' homes that
8 receives a wage in an amount greater than twenty-two dollars
9 per hour before January 1, 2025, shall be entitled to that
10 wage on and after January 1, 2025. The wage established by
11 this section shall be adjusted annually on January 1, 2026,
12 and on January first of successive years, by the same
13 percentage as the increase or decrease in the general price
14 level as measured by the Consumer Price Index for All Urban
15 Consumers (CPI-U) or successor index as published by the
16 United States Department of Labor or its successor agency,
17 with the amount of the wage increase or decrease rounded to

18 the nearest five cents. Any change in the wage amount shall
19 be calculated and published by the commission.
