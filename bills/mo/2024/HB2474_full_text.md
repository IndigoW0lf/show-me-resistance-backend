AN ACT
To amendchapter 196, RSMo, by addingthereto one new section relatingto food safety, with
penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 196, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 196.188, to read as follows:
196.188. 1. Beginning January 1, 2027, and continuing thereafter, a person or
2 entity shall not manufacture, sell, deliver, distribute, hold, or offer for sale in commerce
3 a food product for human consumption that contains any of the following substances:
4 (1) Brominated vegetable oil (Chemical Abstracts Service number 8016-94-2);
5 (2) Potassium bromate (Chemical Abstracts Service number 7758-01-2);
6 (3) Propylparaben (Chemical Abstracts Service number 94-13-3); and
7 (4) Red dye 3 (Chemical Abstracts Service number 16423-68-0).
8 2. Upon an action brought by the attorney general, a city attorney, a county
9 counselor, or a county prosecuting or circuit attorney, a person or entity that violates
10 subsection 1 of this section shall be liable for a civil penalty not to exceed five thousand
11 dollars for a first violation and not to exceed ten thousand dollars for each subsequent
12 violation.
13 3. This section does not impair or impede any other rights, causes of action,
14 claims,ordefensesavailableunderanyotherlaw. Theremedies providedinthissection
15 are cumulative with any other remedies available under any other law.
